John the Evangelist

  • Author: John (most likely the Apostle but possibly a follower of the Apostle)
  • Written for: New Christians
  • Date: most likely in the year 90
  • Develops a Christology (explanation of Christ's nature and origin)
  • Last Gospel written

Sections

  • Book of Signs - tells of Jesus' ministry, focusing on the meaning and significance of "miraculous" works
    • Not meant to astonish onlookers but point out that His Glory comes from God's presence within him
  • Book of Glory - glorification of Jesus through his crucifixion and Resurrection

Eight Signs (Not miracles)

  • Water into wine
    • Only in John
  • Feeding of 5000
    • All 4
  • Jesus walks on water
    • Matthew, Mark, and John
  • Catching of fish
    • Luke and John
  • Healing official's son
    • John
  • Healing paralytic
    • John
  • Restores sight to blind man
    • John
  • Raises Lazarus
    • John

The first "miracle"

  • Wedding at Cana

Cana

  • Location of Cana is not known for certain
  • Most traditional site is Kafr Kanna
    • Located on main road between 2 important cities; not far from Nazareth
    • Ruins indicate presence of a town during time of Jesus (Persian to Byzantine periods)
    • No runnning spring water; water had to be brought from cistersn or from valley and stored in stone jars like in story
    • Ancient graffiti seen on one of the grottoes

I am Statements

  • 4 forms
    • Jesus identifies with something already known to listeners
    • Emphasize relationships between Jesus and followers
    • Declarations of Jesus as life giver
    • Response to questions of self identification

7 Statements

  • I am bread of life
  • I am the true vine and you are the branches
  • I am light of the world
  • I am the resurrection and way of life
  • I am the way the truth and the life
  • I am the fate for the sheep
  • I am the Good shepherd

Similarities between synoptic and John's Gospel

  • Preaching of John the Baptist
  • Feeding the 5000
  • Triumphal entry into Jerusalem
  • Foretelling of betrayal of Jesus
  • Prediction of Peter's denial
  • Arrest of Jesus
  • Jesus before Pilate
  • Sentencing of Jesus
  • Crucifixion, death and burial
  • Resurrection

Contrast between John and Synoptics

  • Ministry in south (judea)
  • Jesus as Son of God
  • Maturing church
  • Long discourses
  • Much commentary
